# Stikkas

Digital products, planners, and printables for creators.

## About

Stikkas is a small independent creative business based in Los Angeles, producing digital products including planners, printables, coloring books, and creative tools. Our products are available on Etsy under the shop name **[thedailytool](https://thedailytool.etsy.com)** and on Amazon KDP.
