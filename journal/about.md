# About

Stikkas is a small independent creative studio based in Los Angeles. We make digital products — wedding planners, journals, trackers, signage templates, and printables — built for people who care that the file prints correctly the first time and the document actually flows the way real days do.

## Who makes the work

The work is made by **Oscar Arana**.

I have spent thirty-plus years in print shops, currently working as a mini-lab technician in Los Angeles. I develop photography film, print crystal archival photos, and run large-format inkjet prints every day. I know the back of a CMYK profile, the inside of a paper-stock spec sheet, the difference between a 100 lb cover and a 110 lb cover at the same brightness rating, and exactly why your wedding signage looks washed out when it was printed RGB instead of CMYK. That is the print side.

I have also spent twenty-five-plus years DJing weddings. Five hundred-plus receptions, somewhere between Long Beach and Burbank. I am the guy at the console who watches the entire wedding happen from the angle no one else gets to see — including the angle where you can tell, by 8 p.m., which couples planned the night well and which couples are quietly improvising. That is the event side.

The products in the shop are made at the intersection. Wedding planners written by someone who has stood at the front of the reception hall and watched the timeline either work or fall apart. Spreadsheets built for couples who need a vendor sheet that survives the second round of estimates. Signage templates designed by someone who knows what reads across a 60-foot ballroom. Day-of binders that match how the night actually flows.

No Canva-flipper on Etsy has this combination. Most digital wedding sellers do not know what bleed is, have not been at a wedding past sunset, and cannot tell you why their files print wrong. We can.

## What we sell

We sell on two platforms:

- **[Etsy — thedailytool](https://www.etsy.com/shop/thedailytool)** — wedding planners, spreadsheets, day-of binders, journals, trackers, and digital planner kits. Instant download.
- **[Amazon — Cute Baby Animals Coloring Book](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0GWMTXPMK)** — print coloring book for toddlers ages 2–5.

Every product is built to a print-shop standard. Files are sized correctly with proper margins. Color modes match the intended output. Fillable PDFs are tested in Acrobat Reader, GoodNotes, Notability, and the major iPad apps. Spreadsheets are built so the math holds when a row is added.

## What we don't do

We don't ship physical inventory and we don't take custom orders. Every product on the shop is a digital download or a print-on-demand book. The reason: we want every product to scale without us. Oscar designs the file once. The customer downloads it as many times as the listing sells. The studio runs in the time around the day job, not in place of it.

We also don't write wedding planning advice we haven't lived. The journal is first-person. The numbers are from receptions Oscar has actually worked. The "what couples cut at the last minute" is the list of what Oscar has actually watched couples cut at the last minute.
