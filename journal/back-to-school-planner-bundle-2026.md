## What a teacher-student household actually needs

The planning needs of a teacher and a student aren't as different as they look. Both are managing a semester-based calendar. Both need weekly planning spreads and a way to track deadlines. Both work on an academic year that runs roughly August through May.

The differences are in the details:

**Teachers need:** lesson plans by class period, a grade tracker for their students, parent contact logs, curriculum standards tracking, a substitute info page, professional development records.

**Students need:** a class-by-class contact sheet (professors, not parents), an assignment tracker sorted by course, an exam prep calendar, a GPA calculator, a weekly study planner.

Building those as separate planners — and then designing them to look like they belong together — is what makes a bundle worth buying instead of buying two separate templates.
