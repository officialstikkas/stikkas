## Start here: the wedding guides

If you're planning a wedding, **[the complete Stikkas wedding planning guide](/stikkas/wedding-planning/)** is the single page that maps the whole twelve-month timeline — the budget, the guest list, the vendors, the reception flow, and the printables that hold it all together. Eighteen minutes of structure from twenty-five years behind the DJ console.

If music is the part you're stuck on, the **[Stikkas wedding music guide](/stikkas/wedding-music/)** is the focused 16-minute companion — the four music decisions every couple makes (first dance, parent dances, must-play, do-not-play), how they fit into the bridge between dinner and dancing, and the DJ workbook that holds them together.

If vendor hiring is the part stressing you out, the **[Stikkas wedding vendor guide](/stikkas/wedding-vendors/)** is the focused 17-minute companion — the twelve vendors every wedding actually needs, the booking order that matters, the four that decide whether the day flies, and the questions to ask each before you sign.

If the wedding day itself is what's worrying you (the timeline, the binder, the coordinator, the kit), the **[Stikkas wedding day-of guide](/stikkas/wedding-day-of/)** is the focused 16-minute companion — the four blocks of the actual day, the three documents that run it, the coordinator who carries them, and the failure modes that crater receptions from inside.
