# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** May 14, 2026
**Last Updated:** May 14, 2026

Stikkas ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website [stikkas.com](https://stikkas.com), the Etsy shop "thedailytool," and related digital product offerings (collectively, the "Services"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect information about you when you interact with our Services.

By using our Services, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the Services.

---

## 1. Who We Are

Stikkas is a small independent business based in Los Angeles, California, USA, producing digital products including planners, printables, coloring books, and creative tools.

- **Business name:** Stikkas
- **Etsy shop:** thedailytool
- **Contact email:** [privacy@stikkas.com](mailto:privacy@stikkas.com)

## 2. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to operate our business and provide our Services.

### 2.1 Information You Provide Directly

- **Contact information** (name, email address) when you contact us, sign up for a newsletter, or submit a support request.
- **Order information** (purchase details, shipping/billing data) processed by our sales platforms (Etsy, Amazon KDP, or other resellers). We do not store payment card details ourselves.
- **Communications** you send us via email or through marketplace messaging.

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website or interact with our marketing content, we may automatically collect:

- **Usage data** (pages viewed, links clicked, referrer URLs, device type, browser type).
- **IP address and approximate location** (used for analytics and fraud prevention).
- **Cookies and similar technologies** (for analytics, functionality, and marketing — see Section 6).

### 2.3 Information from Third Parties

We may receive limited information from:

- **Marketplaces** (Etsy, Amazon KDP) — order confirmations, buyer messages, and aggregated sales data.
- **Marketing platforms** (Pinterest, Google Analytics, social platforms) — aggregated analytics about how users discover and engage with our content.
- **Payment processors** — transaction confirmations (no payment card data).

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use information for the following purposes:

- **Fulfill orders and provide products** (deliver digital downloads, send order updates).
- **Respond to inquiries** and provide customer support.
- **Improve our products and Services** (analyze usage, fix issues, develop new offerings).
- **Marketing communications** (only with your consent, or to existing customers about similar products).
- **Comply with legal obligations** (tax, accounting, regulatory requirements).
- **Protect our business** (prevent fraud, enforce terms of service).

We do **not** sell your personal information to third parties.

---

## 4. How We Share Your Information

We share information only as described below:

- **Service providers** who help us operate our business (e.g., email hosting, analytics, payment processors, marketplace platforms). These providers are bound by confidentiality obligations.
- **Marketplaces and platforms** (Etsy, Amazon KDP, Pinterest) — to the extent required to deliver products or run marketing campaigns.
- **Legal requirements** — when required by law, subpoena, court order, or to protect our rights and safety.
- **Business transfers** — if Stikkas is acquired, merged, or sells assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

## 8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

- **Access** — request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
- **Correction** — request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
- **Deletion** — request that we delete your personal information.
- **Opt-out** — opt out of marketing communications at any time.
- **Data portability** — request a copy of your data in a portable format.
- **Withdraw consent** — withdraw any consent previously given.

To exercise these rights, contact us at [privacy@stikkas.com](mailto:privacy@stikkas.com). We will respond within 30 days.

### California Residents (CCPA/CPRA)

California residents have additional rights, including the right to know what personal information we collect and to opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information.

### EU/UK Residents (GDPR/UK GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area or the United Kingdom, you have rights under GDPR/UK GDPR. Our legal bases for processing include consent, contract performance, legitimate interest, and legal obligation.
